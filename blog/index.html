Korean Progress Update - 2000 Words

July 20, 2020

In this first update I have put down my stats at the 2000 known words mark of learning Korean. I have picked a variety of texts from many different sources and levels for the collection of data. Every 1000 words I will post a new table comparing my current vocabulary to these same texts. In order to minimise creating bias in the data, I will avoid studying from any of these texts directly.
